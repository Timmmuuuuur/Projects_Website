🧮 Quantitative Algorithms Implemented

📈 Returns Analysis

Calculation of daily, weekly, and monthly returns using both arithmetic and logarithmic methods. Includes cumulative return tracking and compound annual growth rate (CAGR) computations.

returns = (price_t / price_{t-1}) - 1 | log_returns = ln(price_t / price_{t-1})

🔗 Correlation Matrices

Pearson correlation coefficients computed between pairs of stocks to identify diversification opportunities and understand portfolio relationships. Visualized as heatmaps for easy interpretation.

ρ(X,Y) = Cov(X,Y) / (σ_X × σ_Y)

⚠️ Risk Metrics

Volatility calculations using standard deviation of returns, beta coefficients for market sensitivity, and Sharpe ratios for risk-adjusted performance measurement.

σ = sqrt(Σ(r_i - μ)² / n) | Sharpe = (R_p - R_f) / σ_p
